Before You Send

A focused communication product for moments that feel unclear, loaded, or easy to misread.

Before You Send helps users interpret incoming messages and check the likely tone of outgoing ones. The product is designed for quick use on mobile, where most real conversations actually happen.

What it does

A compact product built around two practical jobs.

Decode incoming ambiguity

The first job is to help users read incoming communication with more clarity, especially when tone, timing, or context feels uncertain.

  • Text input
  • Screenshot-based interpretation
  • Voice note transcription flows

Check outgoing tone

The second job is to help users understand how their own message may land before pressing send.

  • Fast score-oriented feedback
  • Mobile-friendly drafting
  • Useful when a conversation already feels delicate

Who it is for

People who pause before replying — and want that pause to be useful.

Ambiguous conversations

For moments where the message could mean several things and reacting too fast may make the exchange worse.

Draft sensitivity

For moments where the user wants to check whether a draft sounds too sharp, too cold, or simply less clear than intended.

Real mobile use

For people who do most of their communication on the phone and need help without entering a heavy workflow.
