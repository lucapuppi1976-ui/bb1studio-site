What is Before You Send?

Before You Send is a focused communication product that helps users interpret incoming messages and evaluate how their own outgoing message may sound before sending it.

Is the product already live?

Yes. The live application is already available on its own subdomain.

Where do I open it?

You can open the app directly on bys.bb1studio.com or use the studio shortcut at bb1studio.com/bys.

Is public pricing already published?

No. The live product is available, but the public pricing and checkout layer is not yet published on the studio site.

Will billing and customer flows be added later?

Yes. The root site and access page were designed so pricing, checkout, and customer handling can be added later without changing the live product surface.

Who operates the studio site?

The root site is operated by Guanabomalu SL, CIF B38413647, with registered address at Camino las Higueras, 7 - Arona, Tenerife - Canarias - Spain.

Where are the legal pages?

The current root-site legal placeholders are available on the Privacy and Terms pages linked from the footer and product pages.

Will BB1 Studio host more products?

Yes. BB1 Studio is structured as a longer-term container for additional focused digital products over time.
