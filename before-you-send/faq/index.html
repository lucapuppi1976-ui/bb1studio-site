FAQ

A few direct answers about the current launch.

What is Before You Send?

A communication tool that helps decode tone and review a reply before you send it.

Is the app already live?

Yes. The live runtime remains on its own domain and opens in a new window from the studio site.

Which languages are supported?

The live app and the studio presentation currently work in English, Italian and Spanish.

Is launch pricing already decided?

Yes. BB1 Studio now has defined monthly and annual launch pricing for Before You Send.

What happens before billing goes live?

The live app remains available. Pricing and access are already structured so checkout links can be activated later without rebuilding the site.

Does the studio site replace the app?

No. The studio site is the container; the app keeps running on its own product domain.
