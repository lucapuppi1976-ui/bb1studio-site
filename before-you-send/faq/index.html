FAQ

Short answers for the first public phase of the product.

What does Before You Send do?

It helps interpret incoming tone and preview how your outgoing message may land.

Is the app already live?

Yes. The live app runs on its own domain and the studio site links to it cleanly.

Do I need a separate upload to update prices later?

No. This pack adds a shared config file so prices and checkout links can be changed from one place.

Is the studio site multilingual?

Yes. The main site, product page, pricing, access and FAQ exist in English, Italian and Spanish.
