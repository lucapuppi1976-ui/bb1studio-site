Studio principles

The studio is designed to grow slowly and stay coherent.

Focused scope

Each product is expected to solve a narrow problem well instead of becoming a broad, overloaded platform.

Calm interfaces

Design should lower friction, reduce noise, and make repeated use feel lightweight rather than demanding.

Selective releases

The portfolio should stay curated. New products enter only when the structure around them is clear enough to last.
